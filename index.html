How Spot Fake News

time Required: 1 minutes
Heat rating: 100% awesome

Ever Read a news article recently? And wondered if it was fake news or not. From now on you will be an expert!

  • Things You'll Need

  • A news article
  • Google
  • Reading Skills
  • Your Brian

  1. Look at the source

    Look at what Publication the news article has been published by. make sure they are some one you have heard of before and know that other people trust. If you don't know them a quick Google search can help you find out more about them.

  2. Check for error's

    Even if the news article is from a trust worthy source does not mean that it is still a quality article. Checking the quality of the article it's self. Things like spelling mistakes and grammar errors can tell you a lot about the legitamicy of a News article

    an example of a parody news site.

  3. Check the Author

    make sure the author is someone who has made quality articles in the past.

  4. Do some research

    Do some research on the topic. if it is a fact other people will have written about it.

  5. Read carefully

    Use your brain if something seems to good to be true than it most likly is. below is a ted talk that can help you decide if still are not sure.
